Lirong Zheng, Ph.D.

I develop genetically encoded protein technologies for scalable recording of cellular physiology across space and time.

My research integrates protein engineering, molecular recording, synthetic biology, and neuroscience to build intracellular systems that monitor and archive biological activity in living cells.

About
